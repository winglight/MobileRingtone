Daily Mobile Ringtone | 手机铃声大全

Version 2.2 | Release notes

Daily Mobile Ringtone is a wonderful tool for your phone ringtone. Find your favorite music and set them as the ringtone of the phone or the special contact and share it with others. Please be a bit patient while downloading data for all media are stored on web.

How-to :

- You could filter ringtone by different languages by clicking on the Language menu.

- You could search ringtone by name, singer, or other any words by clicking on the Search menu.

- You could play the ringtone by clicking it in the list of musics and long pressing an item of the list would show the context menu to set ringtones or share it.

- You could play, pause or stop the music by clicking the buttons on the top of the screen.

- You could go to the category of ringtones by clicking the spinner on the left-top of the screen, and order the list by clicking the spinner on the right-top of the screen.
